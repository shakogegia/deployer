Deploy your apps painlessly

The one solution for any kind of deployment. Create jobs once and deploy anytime from your workstation.

Sometimes you have a side project and you need simple solution to deploy your changes. There are some deployment services you can pay and will get great service, but for side project, which doesn't have income, it's not an option. Also sometimes you are lazy and dont do TDD and want just deploy new version. Thats where Deployer shines. No more SSH-into-server and manually doing updates. Just click play button and your app is ready to go!

Configure your servers

Add you server credentials.

Add your server configuration: Hostname and Username. Deployer will use SSH to connect your servers. You must configure SSH key manually

Choose deployement source

Enter your repository details.

Choose your git provider: Github or Bitbucket, enter your username and choose repository. . Deployer will use SSH to pull your repository. You must configure SSH access manually

Add build options

Customize your build options

Enter your build directory on server. Sometimes you want to run command after uodating files on your server, like: npm install, pm2 reload all, php artisan migrate or something like that.

Get notified

Deployer will notify you.

After build completed, Deployer will let you know job build status. Also you can enter your slack token and slack channel id and Deployer will send message to slack channel.

  • 1
    Add server

    Add server providers as many as you want. (Digital Ocean, AWS, Heroku, etc.)

  • 2
    Create job

    Create job for repository you want to deply to your server. You can add jobs to your servers as many as you want.

  • 3
    Enjoy your life

    Thats it! Your are ready to go!
